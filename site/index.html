Start here

You don't need all eight tools. You need one question and one command.

1. Run npx @grainulator/wheat init in any directory
2. Answer the question: what decision are you trying to make?
3. Research, challenge, witness. Let the evidence compile.
4. Run /brief when you're ready. Ship the decision.

Philosophy

Satisficing over maximizing

You will never have perfect information. The goal is enough evidence to make a defensible decision, not a perfect one.

Claims over opinions

Every finding is a typed claim with an evidence tier. "I think" becomes a claim ID with provenance you can trace.

Adversarial pressure

Comfortable agreement is the enemy of good decisions. Challenge everything. Look for blind spots. Stress-test the claims.

Zero dependencies

Node built-ins only. No supply chain anxiety. No left-pad. The tools ship what they need and nothing more.

The name comes last. You build the crop, the steward, the orchard, the mill, the silo, the harvest, the field — and only then do you name the machine.
